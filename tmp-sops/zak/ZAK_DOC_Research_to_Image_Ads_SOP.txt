🔍 STEP 1: Understand the Priority Pyramid

Before brainstorming any ad:

Level of Awareness: Identify your avatar level of awareness; This ensures you're not creating for the wrong level.

Example: Solution aware avatar? Then don't run "feature-focused" image ads like "easy to use supplement."

Emotional Trigger: Open your Roadmap Sheet and start with the top 💣 Emotional Triggers.

Pick the most raw and painful emotional state.

Example:

Emotional Trigger: Identity Loss ➔ “I used to have SUPER thick hair... it was the only part of me that made me feel beautiful.”

🔊 Rule: Pick ONE emotion or Two and start brainstorming



💡 STEP 2: Pick the Core Desire to Whisper

Match your emotional trigger to a Core Desi- from the Roadmap Sheet.

Example Desires:

Regrow my hair

Stop daily hair shedding

Restore my confidence

Early Batches Focus:

On the core desire: → To feel feminine, and worthy again, basically its not about hair its not about DHT, it's about the stolen identity, her femininity, beauty, pride, ability to be seen and noticed, that she is worthy of love

Important: Even if the headline and image focus on pain, the subconscious backdrop must be HOPE.

🔊 Why:

Pain alone feels depressing and pushes people away.

Pain ➔ Hope transition keeps them emotionally open and ready to click.



🛠️ STEP 3: Brainstorm Scroll-Stopping Visuals

Use your Avatar Research to brainstorm visual scenes.

Ask:

"What visual would instantly show the pain?"

"What visual would instantly show hope?"

Examples:

Pain Visual

Hope Visual

Staring sadly into mirror, touching thinning hair

Smiling softly at herself in the mirror

Covering hair with hats, hiding

Letting hair fall naturally with pride

Googling "why is my hair falling out?"

Reading calmly "Understanding PCOS Hair Loss"

Crying after clumps fall out

Seeing almost no shedding while brushing

🔊 Reminder:

No cinematic perfection needed.

Ads should feel raw, relatable, and human like.



🖋️ STEP 4: Craft Short, Emotional Headlines (1–2 lines)

Formula Options:

[Pain Confession] ➔ "I hated looking in the mirror."

[Internal Blame] ➔ "Maybe it’s my fault… I thought."

[Hope Spark] ➔ "It wasn’t too late after all."

🔊 Rules:

Big, bold font.

1–2 lines only.

Emotion FIRST. Clarity second.



🔧 STEP 5: Add Minimal Supporting Text (if needed)

(Use only if extra clarification is truly needed.)

Examples:

"The real reason PCOS steals your hair — and how to fight back naturally."

"Balance your hormones. Reclaim your beauty."

🔊 Rule: Main headline must carry 90% of the ad's emotional weight.



👉 STEP 6: Match CTA Style to Emotional State

CTA Style Matrix:

Emotional Trigger

CTA Tone

Hopelessness

"See how real healing starts."

Shame

"You’re not alone. Learn more."

Fear

"Your body deserves real support."

🔊 Soft, emotional CTAs only. No "BUY NOW" style on top-of-funnel image ads.



🛠️ STEP 7: Build Assets for Visuals

Use AI tools (DALL-E, MidJourney, Sora) to create scenes matching your pain/hope visuals.

OR study top DTC brands for emotional storytelling image structures (example: Nutrafol, Hers, Moon Juice).

🔊 Always anchor visuals to real Avatar Research. Not random "pretty" photos.
